Paros Olive Oil

Paros Olive Oil an Olive grove base in Paros, Greece has been producing high quality extra virgin olive oil for generations,
they take pride with sustainable farming practices and hand-picked olives ensuring the olive oil is pure. with a mission
to share the taste of Greece with the world, one bottle at a time.

In need of a label design that proudly represents Greece as the origin and pays tribute to the olives.
Where we created a logo that is simple yet bold, with the inspired Old Greek borders add elegance and sophistication
to the brand. These borders have a timeless appeal, reflecting the rich cultural heritage of Greece and adding
a touch of artistic beauty to the overall design.
