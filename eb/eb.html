Ethereal Beauty

Ethereal Beauty is a luxury cosmetic brand that focuses on enhancing natural beauty while prioritizing sustainability and inclusivity.
The brand offers a range of high-quality cosmetics, embracing eco-friendly practices and ethically sourced ingredients.
Ethereal Beauty aims to provide a luxurious and sensorial experience to its customers, promoting self-expression and celebrating individuality.

The brand is seeking design services to develop a brand identity that reflects its values and aesthetics.
This includes creating a captivating logo, selecting elegant typography, defining a harmonious color palette,
and creating engaging photography and graphics. Ethereal Beauty desires a brand identity that
stands out in the market, exuding elegance, sophistication, and a connection with nature. The overall goal is
to establish Ethereal Beauty as a renowned for its commitment to sustainability, inclusivity, and empowering individuals to embrace their natural beauty.
