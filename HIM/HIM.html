Harmony In Motion

Moving in Harmony and Igniting Flair Everywhere with their Dance. Harmony in Motion is a Dance Studio that aims to
inspire and nurture individuals through the art of dance, fostering a sense of harmony, creativity, and self-expression.

The symbol is inspired of Yin and Yang a concept derived from ancient Chinese philosophy and is known for representing
complementary forces or energies that exist in harmony and balance. There is a distinct relationship within the fluidity
and balance through the art of dance represent the harmonious
interplay between contrasting forces, much like the dance of yin and yang.

In addition to the logo, the dance studio require a merch design, business cards, flyers, and brochures
corporating elements from the logo and showcasing the studio's dedication to dance.
